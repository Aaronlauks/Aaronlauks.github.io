About SP

Singapore Polytechnic (SP) is one of the leading polytechnics in Singapore and is the first polytechnic established in the country. It was founded in 1954 and has since grown to become one of the largest and most well-known institutions for tertiary education in Singapore.

Common Core Curriculum

Some diplomas in SP offers this common core curriculum which allows students to learn more about the SG Green Plan and the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals. This will help get them to come up with their own solutions to these sustainability issues.

Species of plants

SP contains around 230+ different species of plants in its campus, including their Brown Woolly Fig Heritage Trees found near Food Court 1.

Green Mark Platinum Buildings

4 buildings in SP have been certified Green Mark Platinum with on-going efforts to certify all 22 buildings in SP by 2025. (T18A, T18B, T3B and SP Sports Arena)

Smart Sensors

There are 500+ smart sensors placed around the SP campus to monitor air quality and track human traffic to ensure the health and well-being of students and staffs.

Smart Offices

Smart offices in SP help moderate the air quality using temperature sensors to ensure the health and well-being of staffs.

Water Dispensors

SP has 110+ water dispensors placed throughout the entire campus to encourage students to use reusable bottles instead of plastic bottles.
